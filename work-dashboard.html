Talent Dashboard

About this project

I created this dashboard during my 3 month internship with Talent Data Labs (TDL), a HR Analytics startup. The dashboard's frontend and backend were done by myself. The dashboard is created for premium-tier paying clients to help them evaluate cultural fit and differences for both potential hires and their employees.

I have permission to share the demo version (work in progress) I created using randomly generated names and data. I highly encourage you to check it out!

Click here to view the dashboard. Note: The initial load time may be especially long due to Heroku (free-tier) dynos sleeping.
